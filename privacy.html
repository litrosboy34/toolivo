Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This page explains how Toolivo handles files, browser data, analytics and advertising.

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Overview

Toolivo provides free online tools for developers, creators and everyday users. We aim to keep these tools simple, fast and privacy-friendly.

2. File processing

Many Toolivo tools process selected files directly inside your browser. This means files are often not uploaded to our server for conversion or editing.

3. Information we may collect

Toolivo may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate region and visited pages if analytics are enabled.

4. Cookies and advertising

Toolivo may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality, analytics and advertising. Third-party advertising partners such as Google may also use cookies to serve and measure ads.

5. Third-party services

Toolivo may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, security, fonts or advertising. These services may process limited technical data.

6. Data security

We use HTTPS and modern hosting technologies to help keep the website secure. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Policy changes

This privacy policy may be updated when Toolivo adds new features, analytics, advertising or other services.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the contact page.
