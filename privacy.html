This page explains how Toolivo handles files, browser data, analytics and advertising.

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Overview

Toolivo provides free online image tools such as image conversion, compression and resizing. We aim to keep these tools simple, fast and privacy-friendly.

2. File processing

Toolivo’s current static image tools process selected files directly inside your browser. This means your images are not uploaded to our server by these pages for conversion, compression or resizing.

3. Information we may collect

Toolivo may collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate region, pages visited and performance data if analytics are enabled. This information helps us improve the website.

4. Cookies and advertising

Toolivo may use cookies or similar technologies for website functionality, analytics and advertising. If ads are displayed in the future, third-party advertising partners such as Google may use cookies to serve and measure ads.

5. Third-party services

Toolivo may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, security, fonts or advertising. These services may process limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data security

We use modern hosting and HTTPS to help keep the website secure. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy when Toolivo adds new features, analytics, advertising or other services. The updated date will be changed when necessary.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the contact page.
