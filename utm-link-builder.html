Create UTM campaign links instantly for Google Analytics, social media ads, newsletters and marketing tracking.

What is a UTM link?

A UTM link adds tracking parameters to your URL so you can measure campaign performance in analytics tools.

Does this upload data?

No. This UTM builder works locally in your browser and only creates the tracking link on your device.
