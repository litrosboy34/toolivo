Online Text Diff Checker

Toolivo Text Diff Checker helps you compare two pieces of text instantly. Paste your original text and updated text, then see added and removed words highlighted clearly.

This tool is useful for writers, developers, editors, students and anyone who needs to compare versions of text, code snippets, notes, documents or website content.

The comparison runs locally in your browser. Your text is not uploaded to a server, making it fast, private and simple to use on desktop and mobile devices.

  • Compare original and updated text.
  • Highlight added and removed words.
  • Copy the comparison result quickly.
  • Useful for writing, editing and development workflows.

What is a text diff checker?

A text diff checker compares two texts and shows what has been added, removed or changed between them.

Does this tool upload my text?

No. The comparison runs locally in your browser. Toolivo does not upload or store your text.
