What Is a QR Code?

Published: June 2026

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a two-dimensional barcode that stores information such as URLs, text, contact details, Wi-Fi credentials, and more.

How Does a QR Code Work?

When scanned using a smartphone camera or QR reader, the code instantly reveals the stored information without requiring manual typing.

Common Uses of QR Codes

  • Website links
  • Restaurant menus
  • Business cards
  • Product packaging
  • Event registrations
  • Wi-Fi sharing

Benefits of QR Codes

  • Fast access to information
  • Easy to share
  • Mobile-friendly
  • Free to generate
  • Widely supported

Frequently Asked Questions

Are QR codes free?

Yes, static QR codes are usually completely free.

Do QR codes expire?

No, static QR codes do not expire.

Can smartphones scan QR codes?

Yes, most modern smartphones have built-in QR scanning support.

Are QR codes safe?

QR codes are generally safe, but users should always verify unknown links before opening them.
