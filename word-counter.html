Free Online Word Counter

Toolivo Word Counter helps you quickly measure the length of any text. You can count words, characters, sentences and paragraphs while writing articles, essays, descriptions, emails or social media posts.

Why use a word counter?

A word counter helps writers, students, marketers and SEO specialists stay within content limits, estimate reading time and improve text structure before publishing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this word counter free?

Yes. Toolivo Word Counter is completely free to use and does not require an account.

Does this tool upload my text?

No. Your text is processed directly in your browser and is not uploaded by this page.

What does this word counter measure?

It counts words, characters, characters without spaces, sentences, paragraphs and estimated reading time.

More About Word Counting

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated using an average reading speed of about 200 words per minute.

Can I use it for SEO content?

Yes. It is useful for checking blog posts, product descriptions, meta descriptions, landing page copy and other SEO content.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The tool works on phones, tablets and desktop browsers.
