What is regex?

Online Regex Tester

Toolivo Regex Tester helps you test regular expressions instantly in your browser. Enter a regex pattern, paste your test text and see highlighted matches with a live match count.

Regular expressions are commonly used by developers for validation, search, text extraction, filtering, log analysis and data cleanup. This tool makes it easier to quickly check if your pattern works as expected.

The Regex Tester runs locally in your browser. Your text and pattern are not uploaded to a server, making it fast, private and simple to use on desktop and mobile devices.

  • Test regex patterns instantly.
  • Highlight all matching text.
  • Count total matches automatically.
  • Useful for developers, students and data cleanup tasks.

What is a regular expression?

A regular expression, or regex, is a pattern used to search, match, validate or extract parts of text.

Does this tool upload my text?

No. The regex test runs locally in your browser. Toolivo does not upload or store your text.
