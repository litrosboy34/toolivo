Frequently Asked Questions

What does SHA256 mean?

SHA256 stands for Secure Hash Algorithm 256-bit. It creates a fixed-length hash value from text or data.

Can SHA256 be reversed?

No. SHA256 is a one-way hashing algorithm. You cannot convert a SHA256 hash back into the original text.

Is this SHA256 generator free?

Yes. Toolivo's SHA256 Generator is completely free and does not require registration.

Is my text stored?

No. The hash is generated locally in your browser. Toolivo does not store or upload your input text.
